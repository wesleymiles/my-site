What do they do?

Families or couples traveling to the Boston area, wanting a seaside experience. They don't want to stay at a hotel.

What makes them different?

Body

A Queen Anne Victorian built in 1881, it offers comfortable accommodations and panoramic views of the town harbor. Step out onto the wraparound porch for an unsurpassed view of Abbot Hall and the town’s huge fleet of sailboats and motor craft. Just five minutes’ drive from picturesque Old Town, this former summer “cottage” is conveniently located on Marblehead Neck. Two of the town’s yacht clubs are a five- or ten-minute walk, and the house is a stone’s throw from Chandler Hovey Park and its unique lighthouse. Swim from a private beach in front of the house....
